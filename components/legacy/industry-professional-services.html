⠋ Common questions

Answers for managing partners.

Does it create attorney-client privilege?

No. XBert is configured for intake only. It explicitly does not give legal, tax, or strategic advice and inserts the appropriate disclaimers. Privilege attaches when a partner takes the consult.

Can it handle our conflict-check workflow?

Yes. XBert captures the inputs you require for a conflict check, books a tentative consult, and waits for your team's clearance before final confirmation.

Is the intake transcript audit-ready?

Yes. Every call is recorded, transcribed, and indexed. Default 7-year retention, configurable. Exports in standard formats for bar audits or insurance reviews.

Will it work with our case management system?

Yes. Native integrations with Clio, MyCase, PracticePanther, Karbon, Salesforce, and HubSpot. Custom case management systems via API on request.

How long does setup take?

Most firms go live in under a week. XBert ingests your intake templates, partner roster, practice areas, and routing rules — then your managing partner reviews the first 30–50 transcripts before you flip the line live.
