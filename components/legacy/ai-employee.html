⠋ Common questions

Hiring an AI employee, in plain language.

How long does setup actually take?

Most businesses go live in under an hour. XBert interviews you about your business, scans your website and existing materials, and writes its own instructions. You review, test in a sandbox, then go live. No technical skills required.

Will it actually sound like us?

Yes. You pick the voice, set the tone, and approve the greeting. XBert reads your existing materials to match how you already talk. You can also clone an in-house voice for an extra fee.

What happens when XBert reaches a limit?

It hands off to your team with full context. The conversation, the customer record, what XBert tried, and what is still open. No one has to ask the customer to repeat themselves.

How is this different from a chatbot?

A chatbot reads from a script. XBert is an employee. It picks up the phone, takes action in your tools, remembers every customer, and learns. You hire it for outcomes, not deflection.
