⡇FAQs

Pricing questions, answered.

What counts as a session?

A session is one phone call longer than 30 seconds, or one SMS/chat conversation with three or more AI interactions. Hang-ups, wrong numbers, and trivial pings don’t count.

Which CRMs and calendars does XBert integrate with?

XBert connects out of the box to Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, Zendesk, ServiceNow, Slack, Microsoft Teams, Google Calendar, Outlook, Calendly, QuickBooks, Clover, Shopify, Housecall Pro, Epic, and more. Custom APIs are available on request.

How does the 14-day free trial work?

No credit card required. Get unlimited sessions during the trial. We’ll help you scan your website, train XBert, and go live in under an hour. Add a card to keep going after day 14.

Is XBert HIPAA compliant?

Yes. XBert is HIPAA compliant, SOC 2 audited, and GDPR-ready. We sign BAAs with our AI providers, encrypt data in transit and at rest, and keep account-specific knowledge isolated from shared models.

Is Agent Assist included?

Yes. Every XBert plan includes Agent Assist with suggested responses, full transcripts, and next-step recommendations — so when your team does step in, they have the full context.

Do you offer discounts for startups or volume?

Yes. Talk to our sales team about volume tiers above 1,000 sessions per month, and ask about the Nextiva early-stage program if you’re a qualifying startup.

How long does setup take?

Most teams are live in under an hour. XBert interviews you about your business, scans your website, and writes its own instructions — no technical skills required.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts. You can cancel anytime, and every plan comes with a 30-day money-back guarantee.
