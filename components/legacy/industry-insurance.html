⠋ Common questions

Answers for insurance leaders.

Will XBert give unauthorized advice or quotes?

No. You define exactly what XBert can quote and what requires a licensed agent. It cites the policy on every answer and routes anything outside its guardrails to a named agent with full context.

Are calls audit-ready for state filings?

Yes. Every call is recorded, transcribed, and indexed with citations. Default 7-year retention. Configurable per-state requirements. Exports in standard formats for filings.

Will it work with our AMS or claims platform?

Yes. XBert reads and writes to Applied Epic, AMS360, Guidewire, Duck Creek, Salesforce FSC, and the major rating engines. Custom AMS supported via API on request.

How does it handle PII?

Customer data stays inside your account, encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not use it to train shared models. SOC 2 Type II audited, GDPR/CCPA compliant. Full security posture.

How long to roll it out?

A regional carrier typically goes live in 2–4 weeks: 1 week of setup with your knowledge base + policy library, 1 week of compliance review with your team, and a phased rollout from there.
