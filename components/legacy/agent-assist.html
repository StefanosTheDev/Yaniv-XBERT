⠋ Common questions

Coaching your team, in plain language.

Does Agent Assist replace my agents?

No. Agent Assist is the coach next to your agents, not a replacement. XBert resolves around 70% of conversations on its own — for the rest, your team handles the customer and XBert handles the busywork.

How do agents control what XBert sends?

Suggestions are drafts, not sends. The agent always taps "Send" or edits first. Supervisors can review what was suggested vs. what was actually sent across every ticket.

Will it work in my existing helpdesk?

Yes. XBert installs as a side-panel inside Zendesk, Salesforce Service Cloud, HubSpot Service Hub, ServiceNow, Zoho Desk, and the Nextiva contact center. Setup is under an hour. Custom helpdesks supported via API on request.

How does it stay accurate?

Every claim XBert generates links back to a citable source — your knowledge base, the customer journal, the CRM record, or your policy docs. If it can't cite, it won't say it.

What about new hire onboarding?

Agent Assist runs in onboarding mode for new hires — every suggestion comes with the source, the reasoning, and a "why this works" annotation. New agents typically ramp twice as fast.
