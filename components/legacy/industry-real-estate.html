⠋ Common questions

Answers for brokers and team leads.

Will it talk about specific listings?

Yes. XBert reads your live MLS feed, so it knows current status, price, days on market, open house schedule, and listing-agent details. It will never tell a buyer a listing is available when it just went under contract.

Does it handle fair-housing language?

Yes. Required fair-housing and equal-opportunity language is inserted automatically. XBert is configured to refuse questions that touch protected categories and route those conversations to a licensed agent.

Can it route by neighborhood, zip, or specialty?

Yes. Define rules in plain language ("Luxury inquiries 02115 → Sarah; relocations → Riley; rentals → Mark"). XBert picks the right agent and books on their calendar.

What about contracts and offers?

Hand-off territory. XBert qualifies, books, and chases — but the moment a buyer wants to talk offers, financing, or contracts, it transfers to a licensed agent with full context.

How fast is setup?

Most teams go live in under a day. XBert ingests your CRM, listings, agent roster, and routing rules — then you test in a sandbox before flipping the line live.
