⠋ Common questions

Answers for healthcare leaders.

Is XBert HIPAA compliant out of the box?

Yes. HIPAA, SOC 2 Type II, and GDPR posture by default. We sign a BAA at contract, encrypt PHI in transit and at rest, and audit every action XBert takes. See the full security page.

Does it give clinical advice?

Never. XBert handles administrative work — booking, intake, insurance, messages — and is configured to refuse clinical questions and transfer to a clinician. You define the boundaries.

How does it handle emergencies?

You define emergency keywords and the on-call rotation. XBert detects them in real time, transfers immediately, and hands the live transcript to the clinician so the patient never has to repeat themselves.

Will it work with our EHR?

Yes. XBert reads and writes to Epic, Cerner, athenahealth, NextGen, Salesforce Health Cloud, and most major EHRs. Custom EHRs supported via API on request.

How long does setup take?

Most practices go live in under a day. XBert interviews you, scans your existing scripts and policies, picks a voice, and writes its own playbook. You review, test, then go live.
