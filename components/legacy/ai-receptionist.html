⠋ Common questions

Picking up the phone, in plain language.

Does XBert sound like a robot?

No. XBert uses real-time voice models with ~280ms latency. Most callers don't realize they're talking to AI until we tell them on the call summary. You can pick from 23+ voices or clone your own.

Do I need to change my phone number?

Never. Forward your existing line, or set XBert as the primary answer with one-click failover. We support SIP, PSTN, and every major business phone provider.

What about emergencies?

You define which keywords or intents trigger an immediate transfer. XBert detects them in real time, transfers to the named on-call person, and hands over the live transcript so the customer never has to repeat themselves.

Is it HIPAA compliant for medical practices?

Yes. HIPAA, SOC 2, and GDPR out of the box, BAAs available, encryption in transit and at rest, and audit-ready transcripts on every call. See the full security posture.

How long does setup take?

Most receptionists go live in under an hour. XBert interviews you about your business, scans your existing materials, picks a voice, and writes its own scripts. You test in a sandbox, then go live.
