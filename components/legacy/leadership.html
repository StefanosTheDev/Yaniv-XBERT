Tomas Gorny

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Founder of Nextiva. Two decades of building the infrastructure customer-first businesses run on — now extending it into the AI employee era with XBert.

Polish-born American serial entrepreneur. Tomas co-founded Nextiva in 2006 and has run it ever since — bootstrapping it past 100,000 customers and a $2.7B valuation without venture-capital reliance. Before Nextiva, he co-founded IPOWER (sold to Warburg Pincus and Goldman Sachs in 2011 for nearly $1B) and SiteLock (sold to ABRY Partners in 2018), and chaired UnitedWeb. Named Arizona’s Top Tech CEO by the Phoenix Business Journal in 2017.

His thesis at XBert is unchanged from the one he’s carried through every company: the businesses that actually keep the economy running deserve technology built for how they really operate — not enterprise tools handed down with the price softened. AI doesn’t need to be flawless on day one; it needs context. XBert is the operator-grade version of that idea, shipped on the same Nextiva rails his team has been running for nearly 20 years.
