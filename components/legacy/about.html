⠋ Why we built XBert

Most AI is built by people who have never answered a phone for a living.

We have. We have answered them, transferred them, escalated them, and missed them. We know the cost of a Friday-night caller who hangs up. We know the panic of a Black Friday inbox at 9am. We know what it feels like to be a frontline team that has to make every conversation count.

XBert is built by people who know that work, on infrastructure that has done that work for 100,000+ businesses for nearly 20 years. It is not a chatbot a startup wrote in a quarter. It is an AI employee built by the company underneath the customer work.

⠋ Our journey

The road from cloud phones to AI employee.

  1. 2026

    XBert ships as the AI employee.

    Voice, SMS, chat, and inbox handled by a single trained instance. Built on the NEXT.IQ intelligence layer.

  2. 2024

    NEXT.IQ launches.

    Proprietary intelligence layer that reasons over the customer journal — every interaction, every channel, every action.

  3. 2022

    Nextiva expands into AI.

    The platform transitions from a cloud-phone provider to a customer experience company powered by AI.

  4. 2018

    Crossing 50,000 businesses.

    Service expands from cloud telephony into full unified communications and contact center.

  5. 2014

    Service expansion.

    Full unified communications launches; the platform handles every channel, not just voice.

  6. 2008

    Nextiva is founded.

    Tomas Gorny founds Nextiva to bring enterprise-grade business phones to small and medium businesses. The mission has not changed.
