⠋AI Safety

Guardrails come before answers.

XBert is designed for real customer work where the safest answer is sometimes an action, sometimes a handoff, and sometimes knowing what not to say.

Strict rule enforcement

Define what XBert can do, what it must never do, and the moments that require a person.

Verification before action

XBert can verify the customer before updating records, confirming details, or moving sensitive work forward.

Escalation triggers

When a request crosses a boundary, XBert collects the facts and routes the customer to the right human next step.

Graceful degradation

If XBert cannot safely complete the task, it does not keep guessing. It falls back cleanly and preserves context.

⠋Q+A

The questions teams ask before trusting AI with customers.

Does XBert use my customer data to train shared models?

No. The approved site claim is that customer data is not used to train shared models.

What compliance standards does XBert support?

XBert is positioned with HIPAA and GDPR alignment, SOC 2, ISO-27001, encryption at rest and in transit, and data residency support.

How does XBert avoid unsafe answers?

XBert uses strict rules, approved knowledge, disallowed topics, contact verification, and escalation triggers so it can avoid guessing outside the boundaries you set.

What happens when XBert is not confident?

It falls back or escalates. Your team receives the transcript, key facts, and recommended next step instead of a blank handoff.

Can XBert access only approved knowledge?

Yes. XBert is built around business-approved knowledge and instructions, including selective URL inclusion and exclusion for source control.

Can XBert handle payments or credit card data?

XBert should not process credit card data directly. For payment workflows, use approved links or PCI-compliant IVR paths where needed.
