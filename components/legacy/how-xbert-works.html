⠋Q+A

The practical questions behind the promise.

Is XBert just answering calls?

No. Voice is one channel. XBert can also handle SMS and live chat, and the point is resolution: answer, book, update, route, confirm, or hand off.

What does XBert learn from?

It starts with your setup interview, then uses your website, uploaded files, policies, FAQs, and connected systems to understand how your business works.

What happens when XBert should not answer?

You define the boundaries. XBert can avoid restricted topics, require verification before certain actions, and escalate when a person should be involved.

Does this replace my team?

XBert handles the front-door work that slows teams down. When a person needs to step in, they get the context instead of starting from zero.

How does it get better?

Your team can review conversations, adjust instructions, add knowledge, and use the patterns XBert surfaces to keep improving the experience.
